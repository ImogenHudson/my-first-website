A little bit about me.

I'm Imogen, a freelance web designer and developer with an eye for detail and a passion for beautiful digital design. My journey into tech started after more than a decade of working as an illustrator and designer. Already experienced in creating beautiful digital designs, I realised coding would give me the freedom and ability to provide clients with exciting creative solutions for their web needs.

Having worked as a freelancer for most of my career, I understand the importance of an effective, impactful website to promote yourself or your business. When I first learned how to build a website, I was hooked on the creativity and freedom this gave me. My aim is to provide a personalised solution for your needs. I enjoy using my creative and organisation skills to build simple and effective websites which provide optimum user experience.
